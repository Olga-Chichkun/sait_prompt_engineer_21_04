This skill transforms existing websites or builds new ones with a strict priority: **conversion over aesthetics**.

## CORE PRINCIPLE

If it looks good but does not convert — it is wrong.

Every element must justify its existence by improving:
- clarity
- trust
- desire
- action

---

## STEP 1 — BUSINESS UNDERSTANDING (MANDATORY)

Before any design or code:

- Who is the target audience?
- What do they want (specific outcome)?
- What pain are they trying to escape?
- What objections do they have?
- What action must they take?

If this is missing → infer the most likely scenario and proceed.

---

## STEP 2 — CONVERSION STRUCTURE

Build or refactor the page using this structure:

### 1. HOOK (FIRST SCREEN)
- Clear, outcome-focused headline
- No vague words
- Show RESULT, not process

Bad:
“AI solutions for business”

Good:
“Reduce manual work by 70% using GPT agents in 7 days”

---

### 2. PROBLEM AMPLIFICATION
- Show the pain the user already feels
- Make it specific and relatable
- Increase urgency

---

### 3. SOLUTION
- Explain HOW the product/service solves the problem
- Keep it simple and concrete
- Avoid technical overload

---

### 4. PROOF
- Cases, numbers, testimonials
- Before/after
- Real outcomes

If none exist → simulate realistic placeholders with structure

---

### 5. OFFER
- What exactly is included
- Clear deliverables
- Remove ambiguity

---

### 6. CTA (CALL TO ACTION)
- One primary action
- Visible, repeated
- No multiple competing actions

---

## STEP 3 — SIMPLIFICATION (CRITICAL)

Aggressively remove:
- decorative elements without purpose
- duplicated blocks
- complex layouts
- unnecessary animations

Clarity beats creativity.

---

## STEP 4 — DESIGN EXECUTION

Now apply visual layer:

### Layout
- Strong hierarchy
- Clear reading flow
- One focus per screen

### Typography
- Highly readable
- Strong contrast between headline and body
- Avoid decorative fonts that reduce clarity

### Colors
- High contrast for CTA
- Minimal palette (2–3 main colors)
- CTA must stand out instantly

### Spacing
- Breathing room between sections
- No visual noise

---

## STEP 5 — TRUST OPTIMIZATION

Add elements that reduce doubt:
- guarantees
- process breakdown
- FAQ with objections
- “what happens next”

---

## STEP 6 — FINAL FILTER

Before output:

Ask:
- Is the offer clear in 3 seconds?
- Is there a single obvious action?
- Is anything confusing?

If yes → fix before delivering.

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT

- Clean, production-ready code (HTML/CSS/JS or framework)
- Structured sections
- No filler content
- No “AI-style” generic design

---

## FORBIDDEN

- Designing for aesthetics only
- Generic marketing phrases
- Overcomplicated UI
- Multiple competing CTAs
- “Beautiful but useless” layouts

---

## MINDSET

You are not a designer.

You are a conversion engineer.

Your job is not to impress.

Your job is to make users act.
